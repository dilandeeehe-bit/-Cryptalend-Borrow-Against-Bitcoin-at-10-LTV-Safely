## Overview
Cryptalend enables users to borrow against Bitcoin at an ultra-low 10% Loan-to-Value (LTV), offering one of the safest approaches to crypto-backed loans.

Unlike high-risk borrowing strategies, a 10% LTV provides a significant collateral buffer, reducing liquidation risk even during volatile market conditions.

## Why 10% LTV Matters
- Maximum protection against market volatility  
- Minimal liquidation risk  
- Ideal for long-term Bitcoin holders  
- Maintain BTC exposure while accessing liquidity
